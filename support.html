Frequently Asked Questions

How does WittyFood work?

Take a photo of any food or describe what you're craving, and our AI will find similar dishes at restaurants near you.

Is the app free to use?

Yes! WittyFood is completely free to download and use.

Why does the app need my location?

We use your location to show you restaurants nearby. You can always adjust location permissions in your device settings.

Are the menu prices accurate?

We do our best to keep prices updated, but we recommend confirming with the restaurant as prices may change.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently remove all your data.

The app isn't finding restaurants near me

We're currently focused on Oxford, UK and expanding to more cities soon. If you'd like us to add your city, let us know!
